# moon: Modeling Obesity in Norway (MOON) Markov Cohort Model

Implements the MOON Markov cohort model for simulating obesity
prevalence, incremental health-care costs, and years of life lost in a
Norwegian birth cohort over the life course. Originally described in
Bjørnelv et al. (2021)
[doi:10.1177/0272989X20971589](https://doi.org/10.1177/0272989X20971589)
; this package is an R port and refactor of the Excel/VBA
implementation.
